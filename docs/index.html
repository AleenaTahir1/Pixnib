features

Small app, thoughtful details

Everything a designer needs from a picker, and nothing they don't.

Pick from anywhere

Not sandboxed to a browser or one app. Any pixel on your screen is fair game: video frames, games, wallpapers.

One hotkey

Press Win Shift C from any app to start picking, and again to capture. Esc cancels.

HEX · RGB · HSL

Copy in whichever format your tool wants. Right-click any color in history to switch formats.

Color history

Every color you've picked is saved and browsable, so yesterday's perfect blue is never lost.

Auto-copy

The value lands in your clipboard the instant you pick. No extra click, no dialog.

Quiet & dark

Lives in your system tray with a minimal dark theme that stays out of your way.

how it works

Four tiny steps

From "ooh, that color" to clipboard in about two seconds.

1

Press Win+Shift+C or click "Pick a Color" in the app.

2

The app hides so your whole screen is visible.

3

Point at any pixel and press the hotkey again to capture it.

4

Done! The color is on your clipboard and saved to history.
