# Third-party licenses

Pixnib bundles curated color palette data from the following open-source
projects. All are used under the MIT License.

## nice-color-palettes
- Source: https://github.com/Experience-Monks/nice-color-palettes
- License: MIT
- Used for: the bulk of the curated 5-color palettes in the Theme palettes library.

## dictionary-of-colour-combinations
- Source: https://github.com/mattdesl/dictionary-of-colour-combinations
- License: MIT
- Underlying material: *A Dictionary of Color Combinations* by Sanzo Wada (public domain).
- Used for: named 2–4 color palettes in the Theme palettes library.

Credit to Sanzo Wada and to the maintainers of both projects.
